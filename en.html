What Coeff does

🏆

Live country ranking

The five-year UEFA coefficient, position changes after every round and the points gap to your neighbours. Your country highlighted, the ranking updates itself.

🎫

What's at stake

How many Champions League, Europa League and Conference League spots the current position guarantees — clear and simple.

🧮

"What if…?" calculator

Pick the results of the fixtures already drawn and instantly see what they do to the ranking. Save scenarios and share them as an image — on social media too.

🛡️

Clubs and their coefficients

The five-year club ranking (seeding in draws), a profile of every club with points per season and all European matches.

📖

Cup history

Qualifying and knockout brackets, league-phase tables, top scorers and assists — season by season.

🔔

News about your country

Push notifications after each round: results of your country's clubs and ranking changes. No account, no e-mail.

Three competitions, one app

Champions League · Europa League · Conference League

Coeff knows the scoring rules — halved points in qualifying, progression bonuses, division by the number of clubs — and computes the coefficients itself from results. Values match the official UEFA numbers.
